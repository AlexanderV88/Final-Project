Fan Favorites
Belian

Belian's standard skill can attack all enemies twice and debuff all enemies. Her special skill attacks and debuffs all enemies, making further attacks focus on her. Has high defense.

Violet

Violet has a high evasion rate which allows him to counter with every evaded attack and gains focus with each attack. Using his special with full focus removes the cooldown.

Roana

Roana special skill heals all allies and grants the party the auto-revive status. When she is attacked personally or by a skill that would target all allies, will heal all allies.
