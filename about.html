Why an Epic Seven fan page you ask?

Well... I'm not typically a mobile gamer, but a couple of months ago I was looking for something to play on my phone that was anime themed, after having an anime binge watching week.

So I went to Google in search of anime like mobile games, and came across Epic Seven.

Needless to say, I have been hooked on it since then. I really like the animated special attacks that the characters have. They have a big anime feel to them.

And honestly, I was having a hard time thinking of what to do for a final project. I was going through so many ideas in my head, and while I was making mock-ups of the layout, I kept using Epic Seven pictures as place holders. Then I just decided to make an Epic Seven fan page.

I hope you enjoy the skill showcase YouTube video!
